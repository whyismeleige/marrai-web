# Marrai Design Direction

## Design Thesis

Marrai should look like a premium AI visibility command center for serious teams. The product is analytical, strategic, and technical. The design should create trust immediately.

## Visual Personality

- premium
- sharp
- editorial
- data-rich
- calm
- high signal
- founder-led
- technical

## Avoid

- generic AI gradients
- random glowing blobs
- fake glass panels everywhere
- meaningless floating cards
- vague AI copy
- too many animations
- cluttered dashboard mockups
- low-contrast text
- startup-template sameness

## Layout Principles

- Strong hierarchy.
- Wide but controlled containers.
- Clear section rhythm.
- Generous whitespace.
- Data previews should look real.
- CTAs should be direct.
- Blog typography should be excellent.

## UI Principles

- Use shadcn/ui as a base, not as the final brand.
- Prefer restrained borders and thoughtful contrast.
- Cards should contain meaningful information.
- Use charts only when they explain product value.
- Motion should reveal state or guide attention.

## Landing Page Direction

The landing page should answer:

1. What changed in search?
2. Why does AI visibility matter?
3. What does Marrai measure?
4. How does the free audit work?
5. Why should someone trust Marrai?
6. What should the user do next?

## Audit Tool Direction

The audit form should feel simple but serious.

The report should feel like a premium diagnostic, not a JSON dump.

Report UI should include:

- overall score
- semantic score
- category score cards
- findings
- recommendations
- page-level breakdown
- unreachable pages
- final CTA

## Blog Direction

The blog should feel like a research and strategy publication, not a basic company blog.

Prioritize:

- readable typography
- strong titles
- topic pages
- author credibility
- structured data
- related posts
- table of contents
- polished code/content blocks

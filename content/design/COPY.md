# Marrai Copy Direction

## Voice

Marrai should sound clear, direct, technical, and confident.

Avoid hype. Avoid vague AI claims.

## Good Copy

- "See how AI answer engines understand your site."
- "Audit your website for AI visibility."
- "Find the gaps that stop your brand from being cited."
- "Measure metadata, structure, content quality, internal connectivity, and semantic clarity."
- "Search is becoming answer-driven. Your website needs to be machine-readable, citable, and clear."

## Bad Copy

- "Supercharge your growth with AI."
- "Unlock the future of productivity."
- "10x your brand with next-gen intelligence."
- "AI-powered insights for everyone."
- "Revolutionize your workflow."

## Landing Page Hero Draft

Headline:

"See if AI answer engines can understand and cite your website."

Subheadline:

"Marrai audits your site for AI visibility across metadata, content quality, structured data, internal connectivity, technical compliance, and semantic clarity."

CTA:

"Run free AEO audit"

Secondary CTA:

"Read the AEO playbook"

## Audit Tool Copy

Form title:

"Run a free AI visibility audit"

Form description:

"Enter your website URL. Marrai will crawl up to 20 pages and generate a scored report with findings and recommendations."

Status states:

- pending: "Preparing your audit"
- started: "Starting crawler"
- crawling: "Crawling your website"
- scoring: "Scoring AI visibility"
- success: "Your report is ready"
- failure: "The audit could not be completed"

Rate limit:

"You recently ran an audit from this network. Please try again in a few minutes."

## Blog Copy Standard

Every blog post should be:

- specific
- evidence-aware
- useful to operators
- written for serious founders, marketers, and SEO teams
- optimized for humans first, search second, AI retrieval third
